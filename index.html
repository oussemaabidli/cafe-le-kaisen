Escape to Nature — Brunch, Coffee & Horses

Caffee Le Kaisen is a peaceful family-friendly café surrounded by green fields and Arabian horses. Fresh food, warm hospitality, and a calm place to breathe — perfect for brunch, family outings, and slow afternoons.

Activities & Experience

Caffee Le Kaisen combines hearty brunch with a nature experience. Watch Arabian horses roam, take short guided rides, and let kids enjoy the playground. It’s a calm place for family photos, casual gatherings, and quiet reading in the sunshine.

Horse Watching & Rides

Short rides and supervised interactions available. Best for families and kids.

Playground

A shaded play area for little ones.

Outdoor Seating

Tables with views, perfect for relaxed conversations.

Contact & Location

Café & Restaurant situé dans un club d’équitation à 5 minutes de Geant Tunis City. We recommend using the map when visiting at night — the approach road can be dimly lit.
